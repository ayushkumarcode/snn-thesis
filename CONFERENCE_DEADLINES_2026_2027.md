# Conference Deadlines for SNN ESC-50 + SpiNNaker Paper (2026-2027)

put this together on 5 March 2026 to figure out where i could potentially submit our SNN for ESC-50 + SpiNNaker deployment work.

there are 5 conferences with deadlines still open or upcoming (after 5 March 2026) that are good fits:

1. **WCCI/IJCNN 2026 Late-Breaking** -- deadline 10 March 2026 (5 days away)
2. **ICANN 2026** -- deadline 16 March 2026 (11 days away)
3. **AICAS 2026** -- deadline 22 March 2026
4. **ICONS 2026** -- deadline 1 April 2026 (primary target)
5. **ICNCE 2026** -- abstract deadline 3 April 2026

plus some later targets: IEEE SiPS (April 12), NeurIPS 2026 (~May), INTERSPEECH 2026 Show & Tell (May 7), ICASSP 2027 (~Sep 2026), ICLR 2027 (~Sep 2026).
