## Executive Summary

ICONS is a small-to-medium, specialist, peer-reviewed ACM conference focused exclusively on neuromorphic computing. It has grown from ~134 attendees in 2019 to 234+ by 2020, and from ~15 proceedings papers per year (2019-2023) to 85+ presentations at ICONS 2024. The only confirmed acceptance rate is ICONS 2023: **13 accepted from 22 submissions (59%)**, but this counts only proceedings papers -- the conference also accepts many more presentations as lightning talks and posters. The review process includes a **rebuttal phase** (confirmed for 2026). Papers not accepted for talks are considered for poster presentation. Best paper awards are given annually. The quality bar is approachable for well-executed student work -- this is not a top-tier mega-conference like NeurIPS but rather a focused community venue where national labs, mid-tier universities, and PhD students regularly publish.
