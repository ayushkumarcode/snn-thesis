# Conference Deadlines for the SNN ESC-50 Paper (2026-2027)

quick reference for where we can submit and when.

## Deadlines Still Open

### 1. WCCI/IJCNN 2026 Late-Breaking -- **10 March 2026 (5 days out)**
- Maastricht, Netherlands, 21-26 June 2026
- 6 pages IEEE, ~50-60% acceptance
- has special sessions on neuromorphic hardware (SS40) and NeuroSNN
- would need to reformat ICONS draft to IEEE 6-page

### 2. ICANN 2026 -- **16 March 2026 (11 days)**
- Padua, Italy, 14-17 September 2026
- 12 pages Springer LNCS, ~50-55% acceptance
- has a dedicated "Spiking Neural Networks and Neuromorphic Computing" special session
- LNCS is well-known

### 3. AICAS 2026 -- **22 March 2026 (17 days)**
- Ha Long Bay, Vietnam, 16-18 September 2026
- 5 pages IEEE
- explicitly lists neuromorphic circuits as primary topic
- selected papers recommended for IEEE Transactions

### 4. ICONS 2026 -- **1 April 2026 (27 days)** -- PRIMARY TARGET
- Chicago, Illinois, 4-6 August 2026
- 8 pages ACM
- APC $250 (ACM member) / $350 (non-member), zero via UoM
- THE neuromorphic systems conference. perfect venue.
- draft already written with all refs resolved

### 5. ICNCE 2026 -- **3 April 2026 (29 days)**
- Aachen, Germany, 29 June - 2 July 2026
- 1-page abstract only (no full paper proceedings)
- bachelor/master students get sponsored participation
- good for visibility, low effort
