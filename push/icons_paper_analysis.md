# ICONS Paper Analysis -- Patterns from 50+ Papers

looked at 8 full papers and 50 titles from recent ICONS proceedings to figure out what's normal.

## Key Benchmarks

| Metric | ICONS Typical | Our Paper | Status |
|--------|--------------|-----------|--------|
| Abstract | 90-135 words | ~153 words | slightly long, could trim |
| Sections | 5 | 6 | OK |
| Figures | 3-7 (median 5) | 3 (text placeholders) | need proper figure PDFs |
| Tables | 2-5 (median 3) | 7 | slightly high |
| References | 25-35 (median 32) | 19 | could add 5-6 more |
| Statistical tests | Rarely reported | yes (p-values everywhere) | above norm (strength) |
| Hardware deployment | ~30% of papers | yes (SpiNNaker) | strong differentiator |

## What Award Winners Look Like
- one clear, focused contribution (not 6)
- novel architecture OR novel application domain
- clean experimental validation
- 3-5 figures, 2-3 tables
