# ICONS Paper Analysis — Patterns from 50+ Papers

## Key Benchmarks (from analyzing 8 full papers + 50 titles)

| Metric | ICONS Typical | Our Paper (Current) | Status |
|--------|--------------|-------------------|--------|
| Abstract | 90-135 words | ~153 words | Slightly long, could trim |
| Sections | 5 | 6 (Intro, BG, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion) | OK |
| Figures | 3-7 (median 5) | 3 (text placeholders) | Need proper figure PDFs |
| Tables | 2-5 (median 3) | 7 | Slightly high |
| References | 25-35 (median 32) | 19 | Could add 5-6 more |
